## Mandatory Preflight (Before Any Work)

Before producing brand output, you MUST:

1. Declare the brand work type (strategy, identity, voice, experience, measurement, evolution)
2. Consult `00_readme/scope_and_boundaries.md` to confirm the task is in scope
3. Consult `01_foundations/` for theoretical grounding
4. Check `Patterns/` for existing reusable workflows
5. Check `Templates/` for starter documents
6. Review `eval/BrandScore.md` to understand quality expectations
7. Review `eval/ReviewChecklist.md` for deliverable gates

If you cannot complete preflight, STOP and report why.
