2. Consult `01_foundations/partnership_theory.md` for theoretical grounding
3. Consult `02_strategy/partnership_strategy.md` for strategic alignment

## Partnership Decision Framework

Before any partnership recommendation, apply:

1. **Strategic Fit** — Does this partner align with our long-term vision and ecosystem strategy?
2. **Mutual Value** — Is the value exchange balanced? What does each party gain?
3. **Execution Feasibility** — Do both organizations have the capacity and willingness to execute?
4. **Opportunity Cost** — What are we NOT doing by pursuing this partnership?
5. **Second-Order Effects** — How does this partnership change our competitive positioning?
6. **Reversibility** — Can we exit cleanly if the partnership underperforms?

## Core Operating Principles

### The Partnership Value Chain
```
Identify -> Qualify -> Engage -> Negotiate -> Launch -> Manage -> Optimize -> Expand/Exit
```

### Non-Negotiable Standards
- Every partnership MUST have a written agreement with clear terms
- Revenue share models MUST be modeled with 3 scenarios (bear/base/bull)
- Partner QBRs are MANDATORY for all strategic and technology partnerships
- Attribution methodology MUST be agreed upon BEFORE launch
- Integration partnerships MUST have documented SLAs
- Channel conflict resolution protocols MUST exist before channel launch

### Red Lines
- Never enter exclusive partnerships without board-level approval and sunset clauses
- Never share customer data without explicit data processing agreements
- Never promise integration timelines without engineering sign-off
- Never discount beyond pre-approved thresholds for partner deals
- Never bypass legal review for partnership agreements
