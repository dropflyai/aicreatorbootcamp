# Investor Pitch Deck Template

## Purpose

A structured template for creating investor pitch decks. Covers the standard 12-15 slide format expected by seed through Series B investors.

---

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title

```
[Company Name]
[One-Line Description]
[Logo]

[Presenter Name] | [Title]
[Date]
[Contact Information]
```

---

### Slide 2: Problem (The Hook)

**Goal:** Make the investor feel the pain. This should resonate emotionally and logically.

```
[Problem Statement — 1-2 sentences, vivid and specific]

Supporting evidence:
- [Stat 1: Size/scope of the problem]
- [Stat 2: Cost/pain of the problem]
- [Stat 3: Trend making the problem worse]

"[Customer Quote]" — [Name], [Title], [Company]
```

---

### Slide 3: Solution

**Goal:** Show how your product eliminates the pain described on the previous slide.

```
[Solution Statement — 1-2 sentences]

[Screenshot or product visual]

Key benefits:
1. [Primary benefit — quantified]
2. [Secondary benefit]
3. [Third benefit]
```

---

### Slide 4: Demo / Product

**Goal:** Show, don't tell. Visual walkthrough of the product.

```
[3-4 annotated screenshots or product flow]

or

[Live demo flow — practiced and polished]
```

---

### Slide 5: Market Size

**Goal:** Prove the opportunity is large enough for venture-scale returns.

```
TAM: $[X]B — [How calculated]
SAM: $[X]B — [Geographic/segment focus]
SOM: $[X]M — [Realistic 5-year target]

[Visual: concentric circles or bar chart]

Key trends driving market growth:
- [Trend 1]
- [Trend 2]
```

---

### Slide 6: Business Model

**Goal:** Show how you make money and why the model is attractive.

```
Revenue Model: [Subscription / Marketplace / Transaction / etc.]

Pricing:
- [Tier 1]: $[X]/mo — [What's included]
- [Tier 2]: $[X]/mo — [What's included]
- [Tier 3]: $[X]/mo — [What's included]

Unit Economics:
- ARPU: $[X]/month
- Gross Margin: [X]%
- LTV: $[X]
- CAC: $[X]
- LTV:CAC: [X]:1
```

---

### Slide 7: Traction

**Goal:** Prove demand with data. This is the most important slide for investors.

```
[Key Metric Graph — hockey stick preferred]

Metrics:
- ARR/MRR: $[X] (growing [X]% MoM)
- Customers: [X] (from [X] last quarter)
- Retention: [X]% monthly / [X]% NRR
- Growth: [X]% MoM / [X]% QoQ

Notable customers: [Logo bar — 4-6 recognizable logos]
```

---

### Slide 8: Go-to-Market

**Goal:** Show a repeatable path to customer acquisition.

```
Current GTM Motion: [PLG / Sales-Led / Hybrid]

Channels (ranked by efficiency):
1. [Channel 1] — CAC: $[X], % of customers: [X]%
2. [Channel 2] — CAC: $[X], % of customers: [X]%
3. [Channel 3] — CAC: $[X], % of customers: [X]%

Sales Process:
[Visual: pipeline stages with conversion rates]
```

---

### Slide 9: Competition

**Goal:** Show you understand the landscape and have a defensible position.

```
[2x2 Matrix or Feature Comparison]

         Axis 1 (e.g., Ease of Use)
         Low ──────────── High
High    │ Comp A       │ YOU       │
Axis 2  │              │           │
Low     │ Comp B       │ Comp C    │

Key differentiation:
- [Why we win: 1-2 sentences]
```

---

### Slide 10: Competitive Advantage / Moat

**Goal:** Explain why competitors cannot easily replicate your position.

```
Our moat:
1. [Network effects / Data advantage / Switching costs / Brand / etc.]
2. [Supporting evidence]
3. [Why this compounds over time]
```

---

### Slide 11: Team

**Goal:** Prove this is the team to execute on this opportunity.

```
[Photo] [Name] — [Title]
[Relevant experience: company, role, achievement]

[Photo] [Name] — [Title]
[Relevant experience]

[Photo] [Name] — [Title]
[Relevant experience]

Key advisors:
- [Name] — [Relevant credential]

```

### Slide 12: Financials

**Goal:** Show a credible path to scale.

```
         Year 1    Year 2    Year 3
Revenue   $[X]     $[X]     $[X]
Growth    [X]%     [X]%     [X]%
Gross Mg  [X]%     [X]%     [X]%
EBITDA    ($[X])   ($[X])   $[X]
Headcount [X]      [X]      [X]
```

### Slide 13: The Ask

**Goal:** Clear, specific fundraising request.

```
Raising: $[X]M [Series X]

Use of Funds:
- [X]% Engineering ([X] hires)
- [X]% Sales & Marketing
- [X]% Operations
- [X]% Working Capital

Key Milestones (18 months):
1. [Milestone 1]
2. [Milestone 2]
3. [Milestone 3]

Current investors: [Names/logos if notable]
```

---

### Slide 14: Vision (Optional Closing Slide)

```
[Bold statement about the future you're building]

[Company Name] — [Tagline]
[Contact info]
```

## Pitch Deck Best Practices

### Do
- Tell a story (problem -> solution -> traction -> vision)
- Lead with data and evidence
- Use visuals over text (one key message per slide)
- Practice until the delivery is natural (not memorized)
- Keep the deck to 12-15 slides
- Have a detailed appendix for deep-dive questions

### Don't
- Fill slides with text (max 30 words per slide)
- Make unsubstantiated claims ("we have no competitors")
- Hide bad news (investors will find it; transparency builds trust)
- Use Comic Sans or clip art (seriously)
- Spend more than 2-3 minutes per slide
- Read from the slides
