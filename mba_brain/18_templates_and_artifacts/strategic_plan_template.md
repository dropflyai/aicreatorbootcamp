# Strategic Plan Template

## Purpose

A structured template for annual or multi-year strategic planning. Designed for startups and growth-stage companies.

---

## 1. Strategic Context

### 1.1 Company Overview
- Mission: [Why we exist]
- Vision: [Where we're going]
- Values: [How we operate]
- Current state: [Stage, size, key metrics]

### 1.2 External Environment Analysis

**Market analysis:**
- Market size and growth rate
- Key trends (macro, technology, regulatory)
- Customer behavior changes

## 6. Execution Cadence

### 6.1 Review Rhythm

```
Annual:    Full strategic plan review and refresh
Quarterly: OKR review, scorecard update, initiative progress
Monthly:   Financial review, KPI dashboard, risk assessment
Weekly:    Team-level execution tracking, blockers
```

### 6.2 Decision Rights

```
Decision Type           Who Decides        Who Is Consulted    Who Is Informed
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Strategy changes        CEO + Board        Leadership team     All employees
Budget reallocation     CEO + CFO          Affected leads      Board
New initiatives         Leadership team    Cross-functional    Board
Hiring (leadership)     CEO                HR, peers           Board
Product priorities      VP Product         Engineering, Sales  Leadership
```
