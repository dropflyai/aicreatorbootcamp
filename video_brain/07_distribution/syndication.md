# Syndication — Multi-Platform Distribution and Content Repurposing

## The Syndication Strategy

Video syndication is the systematic distribution of video content across multiple platforms, formats, and channels to maximize reach, engagement, and ROI from each piece of content produced. The cost of video production is front-loaded (scripting, shooting, editing), while the marginal cost of additional distribution is low. Effective syndication extracts 5-10x more value from each production investment through strategic repurposing and platform-native adaptation.

### Distribution Timing Strategy

Not all platforms should receive content simultaneously. Strategic sequencing maximizes platform-specific algorithm rewards:

| Sequence | Platform | Timing | Rationale |
|----------|----------|--------|-----------|
| 1st (Primary) | YouTube | Day 0 | Establishes canonical source; YouTube rewards fresh uploads |
| 2nd (Short-form) | TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts | Day 0-1 | Short-form clips drive discovery and traffic to long-form |
| 3rd (Professional) | LinkedIn | Day 1-2 | Professional audience peaks mid-week |
| 4th (Embedded) | Blog + Website | Day 2-3 | SEO benefit from embedded video + written content |
| 5th (Email) | Email newsletter | Day 3-7 | Drives engaged audience to platform views |
| 6th (Syndication) | Third-party sites, podcast | Day 7-14 | Extended reach after primary platforms are served |

## 2. Long-Form to Short-Form Repurposing

### The Repurposing Pipeline

Every long-form video (8+ minutes) should yield multiple short-form derivatives:

**Extraction Framework:**
```
60-Minute Source Video
│
├── Key Insight Clips (5-8 clips, 30-90 seconds each)
│   └── Standalone value, no context needed
│
├── Highlight Reel (1 clip, 2-3 minutes)
│   └── Best moments compiled, overview of full content
│
├── Quote/Sound Bite Clips (8-12 clips, 15-30 seconds each)
│   └── Single powerful statements, opinions, data points
│
├── Tutorial Snippets (3-5 clips, 60-120 seconds each)
│   └── Complete mini-tutorials extracted from longer how-to
│
├── Behind-the-Scenes (2-3 clips, 15-60 seconds)
│   └── Production moments, bloopers, setup (humanizing content)
│
└── Teaser/Trailer (1 clip, 30-60 seconds)
    └── Designed to drive traffic to full video
```

### Clip Selection Criteria

Not every moment is worth extracting. Apply these criteria:

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Standalone clarity | 30% | Does the clip make sense without context? |
| Emotional hook | 25% | Does the clip provoke curiosity, surprise, agreement, or debate? |
| Visual interest | 15% | Is there visual variety, motion, or demonstration? |
| Quotability | 15% | Is there a memorable phrase or data point? |
| Platform fit | 15% | Does the clip format match the target platform norms? |

### Short-Form Adaptation Checklist

When converting a long-form clip to short-form:
- [ ] Aspect ratio converted (16:9 to 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- [ ] Hook added in first 1-3 seconds (text overlay or re-edited opening)
- [ ] Subtitles/captions added (burned in, not just platform-generated)
- [ ] Pacing tightened (remove pauses, filler, slow transitions)
- [ ] CTA adapted for platform (follow, subscribe, comment, save)
- [ ] Platform-native features used (trending sounds, hashtags, stickers)
- [ ] Thumbnail/cover frame designed for platform
- [ ] Source/full-version reference included (pinned comment or description)

## 3. OTT and Connected TV Distribution

### What Is OTT?

Over-the-top (OTT) content is delivered directly to viewers via internet streaming, bypassing traditional cable or satellite. Connected TV (CTV) is the device layer — smart TVs, Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, gaming consoles.

### OTT Distribution Options

| Platform | Model | Audience | Best For | Requirements |
|----------|-------|----------|----------|-------------|
| YouTube (CTV app) | Free + ads | Massive, broad | All content | YouTube channel (free) |
| Roku Channel | AVOD (ad-supported) | US households | Branded content channels | Apply to Roku Direct Publisher |
| Amazon Freevee | AVOD | Amazon ecosystem | Series content | Amazon Video Direct ($99/yr) |
| Apple TV+ | SVOD (subscriber) | Premium audience | Premium original content | Apple partnership (selective) |
| Vimeo OTT | Direct-to-consumer | Niche communities | Courses, communities | Vimeo Enterprise ($) |
| Wistia Channels | Gated/branded | B2B prospects | Thought leadership libraries | Wistia Advanced ($) |

### CTV Advertising

CTV ad spending reached $25B+ in 2024, growing 20%+ annually. CTV ads combine TV-scale reach with digital targeting and measurement.

| Format | Duration | Targeting | CPM Range |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Pre-roll | 15-30 seconds | Household, demographic, interest | $20-40 |
| Mid-roll | 15-30 seconds | Same | $25-45 |
| Interactive | 15-30s + overlay | Same + interactive engagement | $30-60 |
| Branded content | Variable | Custom placement | Negotiated |

## 4. Video Hosting: YouTube vs. Vimeo vs. Wistia

### Platform Comparison

| Feature | YouTube | Vimeo | Wistia |
|---------|---------|-------|--------|
| **Cost** | Free | $20-75/mo (Pro-Business) | $19-319/mo |
| **Discovery** | Massive (2B+ MAU) | Minimal | None (private hosting) |
| **Monetization** | Ad revenue sharing | Tip jar, OTT, pay-per-view | No direct; enables lead gen |
| **Branding** | YouTube branded player | Customizable player | Fully brandable player |
| **Analytics** | Good (YouTube Studio) | Good | Excellent (lead-level) |
| **Privacy** | Public by default | Privacy controls, password | Full privacy, domain restrict |
| **Player quality** | Good, ads on free | Excellent, no ads | Excellent, no ads |
| **SEO value** | Very high (Google owned) | Moderate | Low (private hosting) |
| **CRM integration** | None | Limited | HubSpot, Marketo, Salesforce |
| **Lead capture** | None | Limited | CTAs, forms, email gates |
| **Best for** | Discovery, SEO, audience building | Portfolio, film, creative | B2B marketing, lead gen, gated |

### Hosting Strategy by Use Case

| Use Case | Recommended Host | Rationale |
|----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Public discovery content | YouTube | Maximum reach, SEO, algorithm distribution |
| Website embedded (B2C) | YouTube or Vimeo | Free + good quality; Vimeo for ad-free |
| Website embedded (B2B) | Wistia | Lead capture, analytics, CRM integration |
| Gated content (lead gen) | Wistia | Email gate, progressive profiling |
| Internal/training content | Vimeo (private) or Wistia | Privacy controls, organized libraries |
| Portfolio/showcase | Vimeo | Best visual quality, professional reputation |
| Social-first content | Native upload | Each platform rewards native content |

## 5. Video SEO

### YouTube SEO (Largest Video Search Engine)

YouTube is the second-largest search engine globally. Video SEO on YouTube requires optimizing for both YouTube's internal search and Google's video search results.

**On-Video Optimization:**

| Element | Optimization |
|---------|-------------|
| Title | Primary keyword first, compelling phrasing, <70 characters |
| Description | Keyword-rich first 2 lines, 200-500 words total, timestamps, links |
| Tags | 20-30 tags, primary keyword first, long-tail variations, related topics |
| Closed captions | Upload accurate SRT file (YouTube auto-captions are 80-90% accurate — not enough) |
| Chapters | Timestamps in description or auto-chapters; improves search snippet appearance |
| Cards + end screens | Link to related content; extends session time (ranking signal) |
| Playlist inclusion | Add to relevant playlist; playlists rank in search independently |
| Thumbnail | Custom, high-contrast, clickable — directly affects CTR (ranking signal) |

**Off-Video Optimization:**
| Strategy | Implementation |
|----------|---------------|
| Embed on website | Embed YouTube video on relevant blog post; sends quality signals |
| Backlinks to video | Include video URL in guest posts, social, forums |
| Social sharing | Share across social channels in first 24-48 hours |
| Email distribution | Include in email with click-to-play; drives early engagement |
| Community engagement | Respond to comments within first hour; engagement rate boosts ranking |

### Google Video Search Optimization

To appear in Google's video search results and video carousels:

- **VideoObject schema markup** on every page with embedded video
- **Video sitemap** submitted to Google Search Console
- **Dedicated page per video** (do not embed all videos on one page)
- **Text content surrounding the video** (Google reads the page context)
- **Thumbnail specified** in schema markup

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "VideoObject",
  "name": "Video Title",
  "description": "Video description",
  "thumbnailUrl": "https://example.com/thumb.jpg",
  "uploadDate": "2024-01-15",
  "duration": "PT5M33S",
  "contentUrl": "https://example.com/video.mp4",
  "embedUrl": "https://youtube.com/embed/xxxxx"
}
```

### Video SEO Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Search impressions | Growing MoM | YouTube Studio, Google Search Console |
| Search CTR | >5% for targeted keywords | YouTube Studio |
| Keyword ranking position | Page 1 for target keywords | Manual search, rank tracker |
| Google video carousel appearance | Present for target queries | Manual search, SERP tracking |
| Organic traffic from video | Growing % of total video views | YouTube Studio (traffic sources) |

## 6. Syndication Operations

### Content Calendar Integration

Syndication should be built into the production calendar, not treated as an afterthought:

| Day | Primary Action | Syndication Action |
|-----|---------------|-------------------|
| Production day | Shoot/record | N/A |
| Edit day | Edit long-form | Identify clip-worthy segments |
| Publish day | Upload to primary platform | Queue short-form clips for next 7-14 days |
| Day +1 to +14 | Community management on primary | Publish 1-2 derivative clips per day across platforms |
| Day +7 | N/A | Blog post with embedded video + written content |
| Day +14 | N/A | Email newsletter featuring video |
| Ongoing | Monitor long-form performance | Repurpose best-performing clips on additional platforms |

### Tools for Syndication

| Tool | Category | Function |
|------|----------|----------|
| Opus Clip, Vizard | AI clipping | Auto-extract short-form clips from long-form |
| Descript | Editing | Text-based video editing, clip extraction, subtitle generation |
| Repurpose.io | Automation | Auto-publish clips across platforms |
| Buffer, Hootsuite | Scheduling | Schedule video posts across social platforms |
| TubeBuddy, vidIQ | YouTube SEO | Keyword research, tag optimization, analytics |
| Frame.io | Review | Collaborative review for syndicated content |
