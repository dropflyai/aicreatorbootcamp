## Slide 2 — The Problem

**AI literacy is the literacy gap of our generation. Most students aren't getting it.**

- The creator economy is a **$250B+ industry** — and AI is reshaping every corner of it
- AI tools like Claude, Runway, and ElevenLabs are replacing entry-level creative roles and creating entirely new career paths — but only for those who know how to use them
- High school students in underserved communities have **almost no structured access** to professional AI tools, real instruction, or hands-on creation time
- Current school curricula are years behind — most students graduate never having used a single AI creation tool
- The result: a widening skills gap that will compound into economic inequality over the next decade

**Westchester High School is a Title I school in Los Angeles. These students deserve the same tools and opportunities as anyone else.**

---

## Slide 3 — The Solution

**AI Creator Bootcamp: Real tools. Real projects. Real skills.**

AI Creator Bootcamp is a **10-session, project-based learning program** where high school students learn to create professional-quality content using industry-leading AI tools.

This is not a lecture series. Students create from day one.

**The model:**
- 10 sessions × 90 minutes = 15 hours of hands-on creation
- 2 sessions per week over 5 weeks
- In-person workshop format
- 20–30 students per cohort (9th–12th grade)
- Embedded in the school's CAP (College & Career Academy) Program

**By the final session, every student has a portfolio, a capstone project, and skills they can put on a resume.**

---

## Slide 4 — Program Structure

**10 sessions. 5 weeks. One transformation.**

| Week | Focus | What Students Do |
|------|-------|------------------|
| Week 1–2 | AI Fundamentals & Image Generation | Learn prompting, use Claude and Midjourney, create original images and written content |
| Week 3–4 | AI Video & Audio Creation | Produce videos with Runway and Kling, generate voiceovers with ElevenLabs, compose music with Suno |
| Week 5–6 | Content Strategy & Platform Mastery | Build short-form video content, write hooks and captions, learn what makes content go viral |
| Week 7–8 | Personal Brand Development | Define a brand identity, build a creator profile, learn how to pitch sponsors and monetize content |
| Week 9–10 | Capstone Project & Demo Day | Produce a signature creative project, present to peers and guests, receive certificate of completion |

**Format:** Each session combines 30 minutes of instruction with 60 minutes of hands-on creation.

## Slide 5 — Curriculum Overview

**What students actually learn:**

**Foundation**
- How AI models work (non-technical, intuitive framing)
- Prompt engineering principles — how to communicate effectively with AI
- Responsible AI use: bias, attribution, and critical evaluation

**Creation Skills**
- Image generation and manipulation (Midjourney, Canva AI, Adobe Firefly)
- AI video production: text-to-video, image-to-video, cinematic effects
- AI audio: voiceover, music composition, sound design
- AI writing: scripting, captioning, ideation using Claude

**Creator Economy Skills**
- Content strategy for short-form platforms
- Personal brand architecture
- Monetization pathways: brand deals, licensing, freelance
- How to pitch and present creative work professionally

**Capstone**
- Students produce a complete creative project of their choosing
- Presented at Demo Day to peers, instructors, and invited guests

## Slide 6 — Tools Used

**Students graduate proficient in 8+ professional AI tools.**

| Tool | Category | What Students Use It For |
|------|----------|--------------------------|
| **Claude** (Anthropic) | AI Writing & Reasoning | Scripting, ideation, prompt building, creative writing |
| **Runway** | AI Video | Text-to-video generation, video editing, visual effects |
| **Kling AI** | AI Video | Cinematic video generation, motion sequences |
| **Higgsfield** | Video Effects | Advanced AI video effects and transformations |
| **ElevenLabs** | AI Audio | Voiceover generation, voice cloning, narration |
| **Suno** | AI Music | Original music and soundtrack composition |
| **Midjourney** | AI Images | High-quality image generation and artistic creation |
| **Canva AI** | Design | Branded graphics, social media assets, presentations |
| **CapCut AI** | Video Editing | Short-form video editing and AI-assisted effects |

**Why Claude specifically:** Claude is the anchor of our curriculum for written creation and AI interaction. Students use it to write scripts, develop ideas, refine their creative voice, and understand how to communicate with AI thoughtfully and responsibly. Its safety-first design makes it the right tool for a high school classroom.
