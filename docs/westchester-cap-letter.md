## What Is AI Creator Bootcamp?

AI Creator Bootcamp is a 5-week, in-person workshop series designed for 9th–12th grade students. Over 10 hands-on sessions, students learn to produce videos, music, images, and digital content using industry-leading AI platforms — the same tools used by professional creators and media companies today.

By the end of the program, every student leaves with:

- A portfolio of 5+ real, completed projects
- Hands-on proficiency in 8+ AI creation tools
- A completed capstone video project of their own design
- A certificate of completion

No coding experience required. No prior creative background needed. Just curiosity.

## What Students Will Learn

The curriculum is structured in five progressive modules:

**Weeks 1–2: AI Foundations & Image Generation**
Students are introduced to AI tools, prompting fundamentals, and the creator mindset. They learn to generate professional visuals using Midjourney and Canva AI, and develop brand kits and thumbnail designs.

**Weeks 3–4: AI Video & Audio Creation**
Students move into video production using Runway, Kling, and Higgsfield — generating cinematic scenes from text prompts. They then produce voiceovers and original music tracks using ElevenLabs and Suno.

**Weeks 5–6: Content Strategy & Short-Form Video**
Students learn how professional creators structure content for platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok — including writing viral hooks, captions, and content calendars using Claude (Anthropic's AI writing tool).

**Weeks 7–8: Personal Branding & Monetization**
Students build a personal creator brand: logo, visual identity, and a pitch for brand deals or sponsorships. This module directly addresses the entrepreneurial and career skills central to CAP's mission.

**Weeks 9–10: Capstone Project & Demo Day**
Students plan, produce, and present a signature AI-created video project. Demo Day is a celebration of their work — families and staff are welcome to attend.

## Why This Aligns with CAP

CAP's mission — connecting classroom learning to real career pathways — is exactly what AI Creator Bootcamp is designed to deliver. The creator economy is a $250B+ industry. Content creation, digital media, video production, marketing, and AI literacy are among the fastest-growing career fields, and they are increasingly accessible without traditional four-year degrees.

This program gives Westchester students:

- **Digital literacy** — understanding and working with AI tools that are reshaping every industry
- **Creative career skills** — practical skills in video, audio, design, and content strategy
- **Entrepreneurial thinking** — how to build, brand, and monetize a creative business
- **Responsible AI use** — ethical prompting, content guidelines, and critical evaluation of AI outputs
- **A portfolio** — real work they own and can share with colleges or employers

Westchester serves a predominantly Title I student population. These are students who have often had less access to cutting-edge creative tools and industry-connected programs. AI Creator Bootcamp was designed specifically to address that gap.
