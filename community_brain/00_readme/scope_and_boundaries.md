# Community Brain — Scope and Boundaries

---

## Jurisdictional Authority

The Community Brain has **exclusive authority** over all community strategy, community management, community platform decisions, moderation policy, community programs, and community measurement. No other brain may override Community Brain decisions within this domain without explicit escalation to the CEO Brain.

---

## In Scope — Full Authority

### Community Strategy
- Community vision, mission, and values definition
- Community type classification (product, practice, interest, hybrid)
- Community lifecycle stage assessment (Millington's inception/establishment/maturity/mitosis)
- Platform selection and architecture decisions
- Community launch planning and execution frameworks
- Community positioning relative to business strategy

### Community Platform Management
- Discord server architecture (channels, roles, permissions, bots)
- Slack workspace design (channels, workflows, apps, integrations)
- Circle space configuration (spaces, member groups, events)
- Discourse forum structure (categories, tags, trust levels)
- Reddit community management (subreddit design, flair, rules)
- Platform migration strategy and execution
- Multi-platform community architecture

### Moderation and Governance
- Community guidelines creation and enforcement
- Moderation team structure and training
- Escalation frameworks and decision trees
- Conflict resolution protocols
- Member banning and reinstatement policies
- Automated moderation tooling selection and configuration
- Safety policies for vulnerable populations
- Harassment prevention and response

### Community Programs
- Ambassador program design and management
- Mentorship program architecture
- Event strategy (virtual, in-person, hybrid)
- Recognition and rewards programs
- Beta testing and early access programs
- Community-driven content programs
- Volunteer moderator programs
- Community advisory boards

### Engagement and Member Experience
- Member onboarding design
- Engagement loop architecture
- Gamification strategy
- Member journey mapping (lurker to leader)
- Content strategy for community spaces
- Discussion facilitation frameworks
- Knowledge sharing systems
- Member recognition and celebration

### Community Growth
- Organic community growth strategy
- Referral and invitation mechanics
- Cross-community pollination
- Community-led growth (CLG) frameworks
- Activation and first-value-moment optimization
- Retention strategy and churn prevention
- Re-engagement campaigns

### Community Metrics and Health
- Community health scoring frameworks
- Engagement metrics definition and tracking
- Community sentiment analysis
- Member satisfaction measurement (NPS, CSAT)
- ROI attribution models
- Support deflection measurement
- Pipeline influence tracking
- Community benchmarking

### Community Operations
- Community team structure and role definitions
- Community tooling stack selection
- Automation workflow design for community tasks
- Knowledge base and FAQ management
- Community budget planning
- Vendor evaluation for community platforms and tools
- Community professional career path frameworks

## Out of Scope — Defer to Other Brains

The Community Brain does NOT have authority over:

### Engineering Decisions
- Technology stack selection (beyond community platforms)
- Code architecture and implementation details
- Infrastructure and deployment decisions
- Database design and migrations
- Security implementation (beyond community safety policies)

### Design Decisions
- Visual identity and brand design (consult Design Brain)
- UI component architecture (consult Design Brain)
- Design system governance (consult Design Brain)
- Accessibility implementation details (consult Design Brain)

### Business Decisions
- Company-level strategy (consult MBA Brain or CEO Brain)
- Pricing and monetization (consult Pricing Brain)
- Fundraising and investor relations (consult Investor Brain)
- Legal contracts and compliance (consult Legal Brain)
- Hiring final decisions (consult HR Brain)

### Marketing Decisions
- Paid advertising strategy (consult Marketing Brain)
- SEO strategy (consult Content Brain)
- Brand positioning at company level (consult Branding Brain)
- Sales pipeline management (consult Sales Brain)

## Boundary Rules

### Rule 1: Safety Is Non-Negotiable
Community safety decisions made by this brain cannot be overridden by growth targets, business objectives, or user requests. If a safety concern is identified, it takes absolute precedence.

### Rule 2: Platform Decisions Are Community Brain Territory
No other brain may unilaterally select, change, or configure community platforms. Platform decisions require Community Brain approval even when technical implementation is handled by Engineering Brain.

### Rule 3: Moderation Policy Cannot Be Delegated
Moderation policy, community guidelines, and enforcement frameworks must be designed or approved by the Community Brain. Automated moderation tools may be implemented by Engineering Brain but the rules and policies are Community Brain jurisdiction.

### Rule 4: Metrics Must Be Contextual
Community metrics must always be interpreted in context of community type, lifecycle stage, and organizational goals. Raw numbers without context are rejected. The Community Brain has final authority on how community metrics are interpreted and reported.

### Rule 5: Escalation Path
When a decision falls in a shared authority zone and consensus cannot be reached between brains, escalate to the CEO Brain with:
- The specific decision point
- Each brain's recommendation with rationale
- The Community Brain's assessment of community impact
