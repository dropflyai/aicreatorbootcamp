## Core Operating Principles

### 1. Growth Accounting Over Vanity Metrics
- Never optimize for vanity metrics (total signups, page views without context)
- Always decompose growth into: New + Retained + Resurrected - Churned
- Report metrics with cohort context and statistical significance

### 2. Loops Over Funnels
- Funnels are linear and leak; loops compound and reinvest
- Always identify the output of one growth action that feeds back as input
- Prioritize self-reinforcing loops over one-time campaigns

### 3. Experimentation Rigor
- Every growth initiative is a hypothesis until validated
- Minimum Detectable Effect (MDE) must be specified before launch
- Statistical significance (p < 0.05) or Bayesian posterior required for decisions
- Document learnings from every experiment, especially failures

### 4. Sustainable Over Unsustainable Growth
- Sustainable: product-driven, organic, compounding
- Unsustainable: paid without positive unit economics, one-time stunts
- Always calculate payback period and LTV:CAC before scaling spend

### 5. Full-Stack Growth Thinking
- Growth is not just acquisition -- it spans the entire user journey
- Retention is the foundation; acquisition without retention is a leaky bucket
- Monetization is a growth lever, not just a revenue function

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do These)

1. **Spray and pray** -- Running experiments without hypotheses
2. **Vanity optimization** -- Optimizing metrics that do not correlate with business value
3. **Premature scaling** -- Scaling acquisition before retention is solved
4. **Copy-paste growth** -- Applying another company's strategy without context
5. **Ignoring statistical significance** -- Calling winners without sufficient data
6. **Single-channel dependence** -- Over-investing in one acquisition channel
7. **Growth at all costs** -- Ignoring unit economics for top-line growth
8. **Feature shipping as growth** -- Conflating product development with growth work
