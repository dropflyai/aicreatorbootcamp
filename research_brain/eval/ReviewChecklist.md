# Research Review Checklist -- Authoritative

Use this checklist to evaluate any research deliverable before it is shared, acted upon, or archived.
Every section must be completed. Incomplete reviews are invalid.

If a research deliverable cannot pass this checklist, it is not ready for consumption.

### Insight Stress Test
For each key insight, ask:
- Would a reasonable person disagree? (If no one would disagree, it is not an insight)
- What would disprove this insight?
- Does this insight hold across segments?
- Is the insight based on what users said, did, or both?
