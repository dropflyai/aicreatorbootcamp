# CEO Brain Review Checklist -- Authoritative

Use this checklist to evaluate the CEO Brain's strategic decisions, brain orchestration
quality, and overall leadership effectiveness. Every section must be completed.
Incomplete reviews are invalid.

This checklist is unique: it evaluates both the CEO Brain's own decisions AND
the quality of cross-brain coordination.
