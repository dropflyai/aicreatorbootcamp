### Core Content: Building Your Portfolio (25 min)

**What Is a Creator Portfolio?**
A curated collection of your best work that shows:
- What you create
- Your unique style
- Your skills
- Your potential

**Portfolio Components:**

| Component | What to Include |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Hero Content** | 3-5 absolute best pieces |
| **Brand Overview** | Positioning, voice, visual identity |
| **Metrics Summary** | Best performance numbers |
| **Skills Demonstrated** | AI tools, editing, strategy |
| **Growth Story** | Before/after, what you learned |
| **Future Vision** | Where you're headed |

**Portfolio Formats:**

| Format | Best For | Tools |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Link in Bio Page** | Quick overview | Linktree, Stan Store, Beacons |
| **Notion Page** | Detailed showcase | Notion |
| **Website** | Professional presence | Carrd, Wix, Squarespace |
| **Slide Deck** | Presentations | Canva, Google Slides |
| **Video Compilation** | Visual impact | CapCut |

**What Makes a Strong Portfolio:**
- Curated, not comprehensive (quality > quantity)
- Shows range within your niche
- Tells a story of growth
- Easy to navigate
- Contact information clear

### Core Content: Planning Your Next 90 Days (20 min)

**Why 90 Days?**
- Long enough to see real results
- Short enough to stay motivated
- Industry standard for content goals

**The 90-Day Plan Framework:**

**Month 1: Foundation**
- Posting consistency (X posts per week)
- Establishing content pillars
- Building initial audience

**Month 2: Optimization**
- Analyzing what works
- Doubling down on winners
- Testing new formats

**Month 3: Growth Push**
- Collaborations
- Cross-platform expansion
- Pushing for specific goals

**Goal-Setting Framework:**

| Type | Example | How to Measure |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Output Goal** | Post 3x/week for 12 weeks | Calendar check |
| **Growth Goal** | Reach 1,000 followers | Analytics |
| **Skill Goal** | Master green screen editing | Self-assessment |
| **Revenue Goal** | Get first brand deal | Yes/no |
| **Impact Goal** | Help 100 people | Comments/DMs |

**The SMART Goal Check:**
- **S**pecific: Clear and defined
- **M**easurable: Can track progress
- **A**chievable: Realistic stretch
- **R**elevant: Aligned with your vision
- **T**ime-bound: Has a deadline
