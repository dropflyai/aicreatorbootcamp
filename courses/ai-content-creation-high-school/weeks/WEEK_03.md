# Week 3: "AI + Human = Better Content"

## Theme: The Human-AI Creative Partnership

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
1. Apply the Human+AI workflow (strategist → drafter → editor → validator)
2. Identify telltale AI writing patterns
3. Add personal voice to AI-generated content
4. Create AI-assisted images and thumbnails
5. Fact-check and verify AI outputs

---

## Session 1: AI or Human? (90 minutes)

### Opening Hook (15 min)
**"AI or Human?" Quiz**

Show 10 pieces of content (mix of AI-generated and human-written):
- Social media captions
- Video scripts
- Paragraphs of text

Students vote: AI or Human?

**Reveal answers and discuss:**
- What gave it away?
- What patterns did you notice?
- Were you fooled? Why?

### Core Content: AI Writing Patterns (25 min)

**Telltale Signs of AI Writing:**

| Pattern | Example | The Problem |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Hedging Language** | "It's important to note that..." | Sounds uncertain, corporate |
| **List Obsession** | Everything becomes bullets | Lacks narrative flow |
| **Generic Examples** | "For example, many people..." | Not specific or personal |
| **Transition Overload** | "Furthermore, additionally, moreover" | Robotic flow |
| **Perfect Structure** | Intro, 3 points, conclusion | Too formulaic |
| **No Opinion** | "Some say X, others say Y" | No personality |
| **Superlatives** | "Extremely important, incredibly useful" | Overselling |

**Live Demo:**
Generate AI content, then identify each pattern together.

### Core Content: The 5-Role Workflow (20 min)

**You Are Not Replaced—You Are Elevated**

```
YOU: STRATEGIST
↓ (Decide what to create, why, for whom)

YOU: BRIEFER → AI: DRAFTER
↓ (Give clear instructions) → (Generates first draft)

YOU: EDITOR
↓ (Refine, add voice, fix patterns)

YOU: VALIDATOR
↓ (Fact-check, verify, approve)

YOU: PUBLISHER
(Final decision to post)
```

**Key Insight:** AI does the heavy lifting. You do the thinking and finishing.

### Activity: The Enhancement Challenge (25 min)

**Step 1:** Generate raw AI content
```
Write a 100-word Instagram caption about the stress of final exams.
```

**Step 2:** Identify AI patterns
- Circle every hedge phrase
- Underline generic language
- Mark where YOUR voice is missing

**Step 3:** Rewrite to make it YOU
- Add a specific personal story
- Replace generic with concrete
- Add your humor/style/perspective
- Cut the corporate language

**Step 4:** Compare before/after
- Share with partner
- Which would get more engagement?

### Wrap-Up (5 min)
- AI gives you a starting point, not a finish line
- Preview Session 2: Image generation
- Homework: Find one AI-generated post in the wild (on any platform)

### Core Content: AI Image Generation Basics (25 min)

**Image Prompting ≠ Text Prompting**

**Image Prompt Structure:**
```
[SUBJECT] + [STYLE] + [MOOD] + [COMPOSITION] + [TECHNICAL]
```

**Examples:**

| Component | Options |
|-----------|---------|
| **Subject** | "A teenager studying at a coffee shop" |
| **Style** | "Digital art, photorealistic, anime, watercolor, minimalist" |
| **Mood** | "Cozy, energetic, dramatic, peaceful, chaotic" |
| **Composition** | "Close-up, wide shot, from above, centered, rule of thirds" |
| **Technical** | "4K, high contrast, soft lighting, vibrant colors" |

**Bad Prompt:** "A student"
**Good Prompt:** "A focused teenage student studying at a cozy coffee shop, warm lighting, photorealistic style, shallow depth of field, laptop and books visible, autumn afternoon mood"

### Tool Demo: Bing Image Creator (15 min)

**Live Walkthrough:**
1. Go to bing.com/create
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Enter a detailed prompt
4. Iterate and refine
5. Download and use

**Tips:**
- More detail = better results
- Specify what you DON'T want ("no text")
- Use style references ("in the style of...")
- Generate multiple, pick the best

### Activity: Thumbnail Creation Workshop (35 min)

**The Challenge:** Create a YouTube thumbnail for your content niche

**Step 1: Concept (5 min)**
- What's the video about?
- What emotion should the thumbnail convey?
- What would make someone click?

**Step 2: AI Image Generation (15 min)**
- Use Bing Image Creator
- Generate 3-4 options
- Pick the best base image

**Step 3: Polish in Canva (15 min)**
- Import AI image
- Add text overlay (big, readable)
- Add your face/branding (optional)
- Ensure it works at small size

**Thumbnail Best Practices:**
- Readable at thumbnail size
- High contrast
- Face showing emotion (if applicable)
- Max 3-4 words of text
- Curiosity gap or promise

### Wrap-Up (5 min)
- Show best thumbnails to class
- Weekly project overview

## Weekly Project: The Enhancement Challenge

### Part 1: Before/After Content Edit
Take AI-generated content and transform it.

**Deliverables:**
1. Original AI output (screenshot or text)
2. Your marked-up version (show what you identified as "AI patterns")
3. Final enhanced version
4. Short explanation: What did you change and why?

**Content Options:**
- Instagram caption (100+ words)
- Video script (30-60 seconds)
- Twitter/X thread (5+ tweets)

### Part 2: AI-Assisted Thumbnail
Create a thumbnail using AI + Canva.

**Requirements:**
- AI-generated base image
- Text overlay that creates curiosity
- Matches your brand style
- Works at small size (test at 100x56 pixels)

**Deliverables:**
- Final thumbnail
- AI prompt used
- 1-2 sentences on your design choices

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't identify AI patterns | Provide annotated examples |
| Enhanced version still sounds AI | Have them read it aloud—does it sound like them talking? |
| Bing Image Creator not working | Backup: Canva's Magic Media, Playground AI |
| Thumbnail too busy | Rule: Max 4 words, max 3 elements |

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **AI is your first draft, not your final draft**
2. **Your job:** Strategy, voice, judgment, verification
3. **AI's job:** Heavy lifting, variations, speed
4. **The best content:** Human creativity + AI capability
5. **Always verify:** AI can be confidently wrong
