# Week 8: "Creating Together"

## Theme: Collaboration and Community

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
1. Use platform collaboration features (duets, stitches, collabs)
2. Find and approach potential collaboration partners
3. Create effective cross-promotional content
4. Build relationships with other creators
5. Understand the benefits of creator communities

---

## Session 1: Collaboration Formats (90 minutes)

### Opening Hook (15 min)
**"Collab or Solo?"**

Show 5 pieces of content — some solo, some collaborations.

For each, identify:
- Was this solo or collab?
- What type of collaboration?
- Did the collaboration add value?

**Key Finding:** Collaborations expose you to new audiences. One good collab can do more than 10 solo posts.

### Core Content: Collaboration Types (25 min)

**Platform-Native Collabs:**

| Format | Platform | How It Works | Best For |
|--------|----------|--------------|----------|
| **Duet** | TikTok | Side-by-side with original | Reactions, additions, commentary |
| **Stitch** | TikTok | Clip + your response | Responding, building on ideas |
| **Remix** | Instagram | Multiple ways to combine | Creative responses |
| **Collab Post** | Instagram | Both accounts post same content | Shared audiences |
| **Response Video** | YouTube | Reference another creator | Discussion, debate |

**Off-Platform Collabs:**

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Joint Content** | Create together, post separately | Both film same video |
| **Guest Appearance** | Appear in each other's content | Feature in their video |
| **Takeover** | Run their account for a day | Post on their Stories |
| **Challenge** | Start challenge, they respond | Tag each other |
| **Series** | Multi-part content together | Episode 1 on yours, Episode 2 on theirs |

**Collaboration Benefits:**

1. **Audience exposure** — Their followers see you
2. **Content variety** — Different from your usual
3. **Skill learning** — See how they create
4. **Algorithm boost** — New engagement signals
5. **Motivation** — Creating together is more fun

### Core Content: Finding Collab Partners (20 min)

**The Collab Sweet Spot:**

```
Too Big (10x your size) → Probably won't respond
Too Small (0.1x your size) → Less benefit for you
Sweet Spot (0.5x to 2x your size) → Mutual benefit
```

**Where to Find Partners:**

1. **Your Comments Section**
   - Who engages consistently?
   - Who adds value to discussions?
   - Who has similar vibe?

2. **Your Niche Community**
   - Search hashtags in your niche
   - Find creators making similar content
   - Look for overlapping audiences, not competitors

3. **Creator Groups**
   - Discord servers
   - Facebook groups
   - Twitter communities
   - School/local creator groups

4. **Collab Matching Platforms**
   - Collabstr
   - Creator Match
   - Platform-specific groups

**Red Flags in Potential Partners:**
- Inconsistent posting
- Controversial content (bad association)
- Misaligned values
- Only wants one-sided benefit
- Poor engagement despite followers (fake following)

### Activity: Collab Prospect Research (25 min)

**Step 1: Research (15 min)**

Find 3-5 potential collab partners:
- In your niche or adjacent niche
- Within the sweet spot (0.5x to 2x your follower count)
- Content style you respect

For each, document:
- Username and platform
- Follower count
- Content style
- Why you'd collab with them
- Collab idea

**Step 2: Share and Discuss (10 min)**

- Swap lists with partner
- Give feedback on choices
- Suggest collab ideas

### Wrap-Up (5 min)
- Collabs are relationships, not transactions
- Preview: How to reach out and create together
- Homework: Duet or stitch 3 videos in your niche

## Session 2: Creating Collaborative Content (90 minutes)

### Quick Review (10 min)
- Share collab prospects
- Discuss: What makes a good collab pitch?

### Core Content: The Collab Outreach (20 min)

**The DM Formula:**

```
[Compliment something specific] +
[Who you are briefly] +
[Clear collab idea] +
[What's in it for them] +
[Easy next step]
```

**Example:**
```
Hey! I loved your video on study hacks for AP classes —
the Pomodoro twist was genius.

I make similar content about surviving junior year
(17K on TikTok).

I had a collab idea: we could do a
"Day in the Life" swap where we each try each other's
routines and react. Your audience would see a new
perspective, and mine would discover you.

If you're interested, lmk and we can figure out details!
```

**What NOT to Do:**
- ❌ "Hey wanna collab?" (too vague)
- ❌ "You should feature me" (one-sided)
- ❌ "I have 500 followers, you have 50K, let's collab" (unrealistic)
- ❌ Spam the same message to 50 people
- ❌ DM repeatedly if no response

**The Follow-Up:**
- One follow-up after 5-7 days is okay
- After two no-responses, move on
- Engage with their content genuinely (not as manipulation)

### Core Content: Executing the Collab (20 min)

**Before Creating:**
- Agree on concept clearly
- Decide who posts what, when
- Set expectations for promotion
- Discuss any editing/approval needs

**During Creation:**
- Communicate throughout
- Be flexible and collaborative
- Match quality to their standards
- Have fun — it shows in content

**After Creating:**
- Promote each other's versions
- Engage with comments on both posts
- Thank them publicly
- Follow up for potential future collabs

**Duet/Stitch Best Practices:**

| Format | Do | Don't |
|--------|----|----|
| **Duet** | Add value, react genuinely | Just watch silently |
| **Stitch** | Make it clear why you're responding | Make their clip confusing out of context |
| **Both** | Credit them clearly | Make it seem like your idea alone |

### Activity: Create a Collab Piece (35 min)

**Choose One:**

**Option A: Duet/Stitch (Solo)**
- Find a video in your niche
- Create a duet or stitch that adds value
- Edit in CapCut
- Prepare to post

**Option B: Peer Collab (Partner)**
- Partner with classmate
- Create a joint concept (challenge, Q&A swap, reaction)
- Film your parts
- Edit together

**Requirements:**
- Clear value add (not just existing)
- Both parties represented well
- Platform-optimized format
- Caption that credits and tags

### Activity: Draft a Collab Pitch (10 min)

Using the DM formula:
- Write a collab pitch to one of your prospects from Session 1
- Peer review with partner
- Refine based on feedback

## Weekly Project: "Collab Content"

Create collaborative content in two forms.

### Part 1: Platform Collab Feature

Create ONE piece using duet, stitch, or remix:
- Adds genuine value to original content
- Shows your personality/brand
- Credits original creator properly
- Ready to publish

### Part 2: Peer Collaboration

Work with ONE classmate to create joint content:
- Concept agreed upon together
- Both contribute meaningfully
- Can be posted to both accounts
- Cross-promotes both creators

### Deliverables

1. **Platform Collab** (duet/stitch/remix)
   - Final video
   - Link to original content
   - Brief explanation of your angle

2. **Peer Collab**
   - Final video(s)
   - Documentation of concept and roles
   - Both partners' approval

3. **Collab Pitch**
   - One polished outreach message
   - Ready to send to real creator

## Collaboration Ethics Guide

**Do:**
- ✅ Give credit always
- ✅ Ask permission when needed
- ✅ Promote your partners
- ✅ Be reliable and communicative
- ✅ Share success fairly

**Don't:**
- ❌ Steal ideas without credit
- ❌ Use someone's content without adding value
- ❌ Ghost after getting what you want
- ❌ Take more credit than earned
- ❌ Collab just for followers (transactional feels obvious)

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't find collab partners | Start with classmates, then expand to online communities |
| No response to DMs | It happens, don't take it personally, try others |
| Duet/stitch feels awkward | Practice, start with easy reactions, build confidence |
| Partner isn't pulling weight | Communicate expectations clearly upfront |
| Collab content underperformed | Not every collab works, analyze why and learn |
