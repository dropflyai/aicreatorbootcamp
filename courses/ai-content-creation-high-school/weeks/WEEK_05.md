# Week 5: "Sound That Resonates"

## Theme: Audio-First Content Creation

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
1. Find and use trending sounds strategically
2. Generate custom music using AI tools
3. Create voiceovers using AI voice generation
4. Edit audio for podcasts and videos
5. Understand the psychology of sound in content

---

## Session 1: The Power of Sound (90 minutes)

### Opening Hook (15 min)
**"Guess That Sound"**

Play the first 2 seconds of 10 trending sounds (no video).
Students guess:
- What platform is this from?
- What type of content uses this?
- Why is this sound viral?

**Key Finding:** Sound is often MORE important than visuals. 90% of TikTok users say sound is essential.

### Core Content: Sound Psychology (20 min)

**Why Sound Matters:**
- Sound triggers emotion faster than visuals
- Music creates anticipation and payoff
- Familiar sounds = instant recognition
- Audio hooks work even when scrolling

**Types of Audio Content:**

| Type | Use Case | Platform Strength |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| **Trending Sounds** | Riding momentum | TikTok, Reels |
| **Original Audio** | Building brand | All platforms |
| **Voiceover** | Educational, storytelling | YouTube, TikTok |
| **Music Only** | Aesthetic, vibes | Instagram |
| **ASMR/Satisfying** | Engagement bait | TikTok |

**The Sound-Content Matrix:**

| Content Type | Best Sound Choice |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Tutorial | Calm background + clear voiceover |
| Comedy | Trending sound with twist |
| Story time | Dramatic music + personal voice |
| Aesthetic | Lo-fi, trending music |
| Reaction | Original sound is the point |

### Core Content: Finding Trending Sounds (20 min)

**Where to Find Trending Sounds:**

1. **TikTok Creative Center** (ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter)
   - Official trending sounds data
   - Sorted by popularity, growth rate
   - Shows video count using each sound

2. **In-App Discovery**
   - Scroll your FYP with sound on
   - Note sounds appearing 3+ times
   - Check sound page for video count

3. **Creator Accounts**
   - Follow creators in your niche
   - Note what sounds they use
   - Look for early adopters

4. **Sound Libraries**
   - CapCut built-in library
   - Epidemic Sound (if available)
   - YouTube Audio Library (free)

**Timing Strategy:**
- **Too early:** Sound has <10K videos
- **Sweet spot:** 50K-500K videos
- **Too late:** 1M+ videos (saturated)

### Activity: Trending Sound Hunt (30 min)

**Step 1: Research (15 min)**
- Find 5 trending sounds in your niche
- For each, document:
  - Sound name/description
  - Current video count
  - Why it's trending
  - Content angle you'd use

**Step 2: Content Concept (15 min)**
- Pick your favorite sound
- Write a 30-second video concept using it
- Include: Visual description, text overlay, your angle

**Share best concepts with class.**

### Wrap-Up (5 min)
- Sound is strategy, not afterthought
- Preview Session 2: AI audio generation
- Homework: Save 10 sounds you like to a playlist

## Session 2: AI Audio Creation (90 minutes)

### Quick Review (10 min)
- Share trending sounds found
- Discuss: Which sounds would work for your niche?

### Core Content: AI Music Generation (25 min)

**Tool: Suno AI (suno.com)**

Suno generates custom music from text prompts.

**Prompt Structure:**
```
[GENRE] + [MOOD] + [TEMPO] + [INSTRUMENTS] + [STYLE REFERENCE]
```

**Examples:**

| Prompt | Result |
|--------|--------|
| "upbeat lo-fi hip hop, chill vibes, soft piano and mellow drums" | Study beats |
| "dramatic cinematic orchestral, building tension, epic reveal" | Storytime background |
| "catchy pop, feel-good summer, acoustic guitar hook" | Uplifting content |
| "dark trap beat, moody, heavy bass, minimal melody" | Edgy/serious content |

**Live Demo:**
1. Go to suno.com
2. Enter a prompt
3. Generate variations
4. Download and use in CapCut

**Copyright Note:**
- AI-generated music = generally safe to use
- Check each tool's terms of service
- Never claim you composed it if you didn't

### Core Content: AI Voice Generation (20 min)

**Tool: ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io)**

Creates realistic voiceovers from text.

**Use Cases:**
- Narration for videos
- Character voices
- Multilingual content
- Accessibility (read-aloud)

**Demo:**
1. Create free account
2. Type or paste script
3. Select voice
4. Generate and download

**Voice Selection:**
- Match voice to content tone
- Consider audience expectations
- Test multiple voices before choosing

**Ethics Discussion:**
- Never clone someone's voice without permission
- Disclose AI voices when required by platform
- Don't use AI voice to deceive

### Core Content: Podcast Basics (15 min)

**Audio-First Content on Social:**
- Podcast clips perform well on TikTok/Reels
- Audio-focused content is growing
- Good entry point for camera-shy creators

**Basic Podcast Setup:**
- Phone with good mic (wired earbuds work)
- Quiet room
- Consistent recording spot
- Simple editing in CapCut

**Podcast Clip Format:**
1. Attention-grabbing quote (0-3 sec)
2. Context setup (3-10 sec)
3. The insight/story (10-50 sec)
4. Cliffhanger or CTA (last 5 sec)

### Activity: Create Audio Content (20 min)

**Choose One:**

**Option A: AI Music**
- Generate a custom track in Suno
- Create a 30-second video concept using it
- Export the audio

**Option B: AI Voiceover**
- Write a 30-second script
- Generate voiceover in ElevenLabs
- Plan visuals to accompany it

**Option C: Podcast Clip**
- Record a 60-second audio take
- Discuss something in your niche
- Plan how to turn it into a video

## Weekly Project: "Audio-First Content"

Create ONE piece of content where audio is the star.

### Requirements

**Choose Your Format:**
- Video using trending sound (with unique angle)
- Video with AI-generated music background
- Video with AI voiceover narration
- Podcast clip with visualizer/captions

**Technical:**
- Audio must be high quality (no distortion, balanced levels)
- Captions if there's speech
- Music and voice balanced properly
- 30-60 seconds length

**Creative:**
- Audio choice enhances the content
- Sound matches mood/message
- Intentional audio decisions (not just random music)

### Deliverables
1. Final video file
2. Audio source documented (trending sound link, AI prompt used, or recording notes)
3. Brief write-up: Why did you choose this audio approach?

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Suno not generating good results | Be more specific in prompt, try multiple generations |
| ElevenLabs voice sounds robotic | Try different voice, adjust stability settings |
| Audio levels unbalanced | Use CapCut's audio mixer to adjust |
| Can't find trending sounds | Use TikTok Creative Center, sort by "breakout" |
| Recording has background noise | Record in quieter space, use noise reduction in CapCut |
