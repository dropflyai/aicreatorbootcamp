# Week 7: "Your Creator Brand"

## Theme: Building a Recognizable Identity

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
1. Define their unique creator voice and positioning
2. Create a consistent visual identity system
3. Identify their content pillars (3-5 core topics)
4. Build a cohesive profile that attracts followers
5. Use AI to develop and refine brand elements

## Session 1: Finding Your Voice (90 minutes)

### Opening Hook (15 min)
**"Guess the Creator"**

Show 5 pieces of content with creator names hidden. Just the style, voice, visuals.

Students guess which creator made each one.

**Key Finding:** Strong creators are instantly recognizable. That's brand.

**Discussion:** What made each one recognizable without seeing their name?

### Core Content: What Is a Creator Brand? (20 min)

**Brand = Promise + Pattern**

- **Promise:** What do followers get from you?
- **Pattern:** How do they recognize it's you?

**The Brand Triangle:**

```
           VOICE
          /     \
         /       \
      VISUAL ─── VALUE
```

- **Voice:** How you sound, your personality
- **Visual:** How your content looks, colors, style
- **Value:** What you consistently deliver

**Brand Positioning Statement:**
```
I help [AUDIENCE] achieve/understand/enjoy [OUTCOME]
through [YOUR UNIQUE APPROACH].
```

**Examples:**
- "I help high school students survive math through relatable humor and quick hacks."
- "I help gamers find hidden gems through honest reviews and gameplay clips."
- "I help creative teens express themselves through DIY tutorials with no expensive supplies."

### Core Content: Defining Your Voice (20 min)

**Voice Dimensions:**

| Dimension | Spectrum | Where Are You? |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| **Tone** | Serious ←→ Playful | |
| **Energy** | Calm ←→ Hype | |
| **Language** | Formal ←→ Casual | |
| **Expertise** | Beginner ←→ Expert | |
| **Emotion** | Reserved ←→ Expressive | |

**Voice Discovery Questions:**
1. If your content was a person, what would they be like?
2. What 3 adjectives describe your vibe?
3. What would NEVER appear in your content?
4. Who do you sound like (but you're the [blank] version)?

**The "[Adjective] + [Niche] Creator" Formula:**
- The sarcastic study tips creator
- The wholesome gaming creator
- The chaotic art tutorial creator
- The chill productivity creator

### Activity: Brand Discovery Workshop (30 min)

**Step 1: Positioning Statement (10 min)**
Write your brand positioning statement:
"I help [audience] [achieve/understand/enjoy] [outcome] through [unique approach]."

**Step 2: Voice Definition (10 min)**
- Rate yourself on each voice dimension
- Write your "Adjective + Niche" formula
- List 3 things that are "very you" and 3 things that are "never you"

**Step 3: Peer Feedback (10 min)**
- Swap with partner
- Do they agree with how you see yourself?
- What would THEY add to your brand?

### Wrap-Up (5 min)
- Brand is consistency over time
- Preview: Visual identity in Session 2
- Homework: Find 3 creators whose brand you admire

## Session 2: Visual Identity (90 minutes)

### Quick Review (10 min)
- Share positioning statements
- Discuss: What makes a brand visually consistent?

### Core Content: Visual Brand Elements (25 min)

**The Visual Identity System:**

1. **Color Palette**
   - 2-3 primary colors
   - 1-2 accent colors
   - Consistent across all content

2. **Typography**
   - 1-2 fonts maximum
   - Consistent for captions, thumbnails
   - Readable at small sizes

3. **Visual Style**
   - Lighting consistency
   - Editing style (filters, effects)
   - Framing and composition

4. **Recurring Elements**
   - Logo or watermark
   - Intro/outro style
   - Thumbnail template

**Color Psychology Quick Guide:**

| Color | Feeling | Good For |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Red | Energy, urgency | Entertainment, drama |
| Blue | Trust, calm | Educational, professional |
| Yellow | Happy, attention | Fun, optimistic content |
| Green | Growth, nature | Health, wellness |
| Purple | Creative, luxury | Artistic, unique |
| Orange | Friendly, energetic | Youth-focused, active |
| Black/White | Clean, modern | Minimalist, sophisticated |

### Core Content: Profile Optimization (15 min)

**Your Profile = Your Landing Page**

**Username:**
- Easy to remember
- Easy to spell
- Consistent across platforms
- Includes niche keyword if possible

**Profile Picture:**
- Clear face (or consistent logo)
- Good lighting
- Same across platforms
- Works at tiny size

**Bio Formula:**
```
[What you do/who you help]
[Proof/credibility point]
[CTA or personality touch]
```

**Examples:**
```
Study tips that actually work 📚
Went from C's to A's using these
New tips every day ⬇️
```

```
Making games fun, not stressful 🎮
100K+ of us vs. the algorithm
Drop a follow, grab some wins
```

**Link in Bio:**
- Use Linktree, Beacons, or Stan Store
- 3-5 links maximum
- Most important link at top

## Content Pillars Guide

**What Are Content Pillars?**
3-5 core topics you consistently create content about.

**Why They Matter:**
- Helps you stay focused
- Builds expertise/authority
- Makes content planning easier
- Attracts consistent audience

**Content Pillar Formula:**
Each pillar should be:
- Interesting to you (sustainable)
- Valuable to audience (useful)
- Differentiated (your angle)

**Example: Student Study Tips Creator**
1. **Study techniques** (main value)
2. **School survival** (relatable struggles)
3. **Productivity tools** (practical)
4. **Motivation/mindset** (emotional support)
5. **Behind-the-scenes** (connection)

**Example: Gaming Creator**
1. **Game reviews** (main value)
2. **Tips and tricks** (practical)
3. **Gaming news** (timely)
4. **Gameplay clips** (entertainment)
5. **Setup/gear** (aspiration)

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't define voice | Record yourself talking about your niche for 2 min, notice your natural style |
| Colors don't feel right | Use coolors.co to generate palettes, test with sample content |
| Templates look amateur | Study templates from top creators, use Canva's professional templates as base |
| Too many content pillars | Narrow to 3-5, you can expand later |
| Brand feels forced | Start with what's natural, brand will evolve over time |
