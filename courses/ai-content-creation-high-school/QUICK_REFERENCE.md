## Platform Specs Cheat Sheet

### TikTok
- Aspect: 9:16 vertical
- Optimal length: 15-34 seconds
- Captions: Essential (80% watch muted)
- Algorithm cares most about: Watch completion > Rewatches > Shares

### Instagram Reels
- Aspect: 9:16 vertical
- Optimal length: 15-30 seconds
- Key signal: Saves (strongest engagement)
- Warning: TikTok watermarks get penalized

### YouTube Shorts
- Aspect: 9:16 vertical
- Max length: 60 seconds
- Key signal: Watch time
- Tip: Can drive to long-form content

## The STEPPS Virality Framework

| Letter | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| S | Social Currency | "I found this secret..." |
| T | Triggers | Back-to-school content in August |
| E | Emotion | High energy (awe, surprise, anger) |
| P | Public | Challenges, trends, duets |
| P | Practical Value | "3 things I wish I knew..." |
| S | Stories | Storytime with a twist |

**Remember:** Low-arousal emotions (sadness, contentment) don't spread. High-energy emotions do.

## Hook Formulas That Work

1. **Bold Statement:** "Most people do [X] completely wrong"
2. **Question:** "Would you do [X] if you knew [Y]?"
3. **Shock:** Show the result first
4. **POV:** "POV: You're [relatable situation]"
5. **Tutorial:** "How to [outcome] in [time]"
6. **List:** "3 things about [topic]"

## The Creator's Code (Ethics)

1. **AI is assistant, not replacement** — You provide ideas and judgment
2. **Be transparent when it matters** — Disclose major AI contribution
3. **Verify before publish** — Fact-check AI outputs
4. **Keep it you** — Your voice, your style

## Posting Frequency Guidelines

| Platform | Minimum | Optimal |
|----------|---------|---------|
| TikTok | 1x/day | 1-3x/day |
| Instagram Reels | 3x/week | 4-7x/week |
| YouTube Shorts | 3x/week | 3-5x/week |

**The Real Rule:** Consistency beats volume. Daily for 100 days > 30 videos in week 1.

## Emergency Prompts

### For Ideas
```
Give me 10 trending content ideas for [NICHE] suitable for TikTok. Make them specific and actionable.
```

### For Scripts
```
Write a 30-second video script about [TOPIC]. Include:
- Hook (first 3 seconds to stop scrolling)
- Value (the main content)
- CTA (what to do next)
Make it conversational, not formal.
```

### For Captions
```
Write 5 Instagram caption options for a post about [TOPIC]. Each should be different in tone: 1) casual, 2) educational, 3) funny, 4) inspirational, 5) question-based.
```

### For Thumbnails
```
Describe a YouTube thumbnail concept for a video about [TOPIC]. Include: main image, text overlay, colors, and emotional appeal.
```
