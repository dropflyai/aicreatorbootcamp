# AI Content Creation for High School Students

## 10-Week Course: Create Viral Social Media Videos with AI

**Target Audience:** High School Students (Ages 14-18)
**Duration:** 10 weeks, 2-3 hours per week
**Format:** Project-based learning with real publishing

---

## Course Philosophy

> **Learn by creating. Publish to grow. Iterate from feedback.**

This course treats students as real content creators from Day 1. Every week produces content that can be published to real platforms. AI is a creative assistant, not a replacement for human creativity.

---

## The Three Arcs

```
ARC 1: FOUNDATION (Weeks 1-3)
├── Understand AI tools and ethics
├── Master prompting and workflows
├── Create first viral-worthy content
└── OUTPUT: Published content portfolio begins

ARC 2: APPLICATION (Weeks 4-7)
├── Apply AI to video, audio, visuals
├── Develop personal creator brand
├── Learn platform algorithms
└── OUTPUT: Multi-format content across platforms

ARC 3: MASTERY (Weeks 8-10)
├── Content strategy and analytics
├── Collaboration and community
├── Sustainable creator workflow
└── OUTPUT: Capstone showcase + continued growth
```

## Week-by-Week Curriculum

### WEEK 1: "What Is AI, Really?"
**Theme:** Demystifying AI for Creators

**Learning Objectives:**
- Explain what generative AI is (pattern matching, not magic)
- Set up accounts on core AI platforms (ChatGPT, CapCut, Canva)
- Write first successful AI prompts
- Understand AI ethics: disclosure, attribution, authenticity

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "AI Already Knows You" — Show how AI powers their TikTok FYP, Spotify Discover, Instagram Explore
- Demo: AI capabilities and limitations (live demonstration)
- Activity: First prompts — ask AI to interview you about your interests

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- Account setup workshop: ChatGPT, CapCut, Canva
- Ethics discussion: When must you disclose AI use?
- First post creation with AI assistance

**Weekly Project:** "My AI Creator Manifesto"
- Visual post declaring your content niche
- Must include: Who you are, what you create, your creative values
- AI-assisted but distinctly YOU

**Tools Introduced:**
- ChatGPT (free tier)
- Canva (free)
- Bing Image Creator (free)

---

### WEEK 2: "The Art of the Prompt"
**Theme:** Words That Control AI

**Learning Objectives:**
- Master the ROLE + CONTEXT + TASK + CONSTRAINTS prompt structure
- Iterate and refine prompts for better outputs
- Write hooks that stop the scroll (first 3 seconds)
- Generate captions optimized for each platform

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "Prompt Battle" — Show two outputs from same AI, one great, one terrible
- Framework: The anatomy of a perfect prompt
- Activity: Transform 5 bad prompts into great ones

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- Platform-specific writing (TikTok vs. Instagram vs. YouTube)
- Hook formulas that work:
  - Bold statement: "Most people do [X] wrong"
  - Question: "Would you do [X] if you knew [Y]?"
  - Shock: Lead with surprising results
- Caption writing workshop

**Weekly Project:** "Prompt Portfolio + 5 Platform Captions"
- 5 refined prompts across different use cases
- Same content idea adapted for 5 platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X, LinkedIn)

**Tools Mastered:**
- ChatGPT prompt engineering
- Grammarly (optional)

---

### WEEK 3: "AI + Human = Better Content"
**Theme:** The Human-AI Creative Partnership

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand the Human+AI workflow (strategist → drafter → editor)
- Identify and fix AI writing patterns
- Add personal voice to AI-generated content
- Create AI-assisted images and thumbnails

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "AI or Human?" quiz — Can students spot AI writing?
- The 5-role workflow: YOU are the strategist, AI is the assistant
- Activity: Take raw AI output and make it sound like YOU

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- AI image generation basics
- Prompt structure for images: style, mood, composition, technical specs
- Thumbnail creation workshop

**Weekly Project:** "The Enhancement Challenge"
- Take AI-generated content and transform it
- Before/after comparison showing your additions
- Create thumbnail using AI + Canva

**Tools Introduced:**
- Bing Image Creator (DALL-E 3, free)
- Canva Magic Media
- Playground AI (free tier)

---

### WEEK 4: "Video That Stops the Scroll"
**Theme:** Short-Form Video Mastery

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand platform specs (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Master the hook (0.5-1 second to capture attention)
- Apply pacing and editing for retention
- Use AI-powered editing tools

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: Analyze 10 viral videos — what makes the hook work?
- Platform specifications breakdown:
  - TikTok: 9:16, optimal 15-34 seconds
  - Reels: 9:16, optimal 15-30 seconds
  - Shorts: 9:16, max 60 seconds
- Hook formula practice

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- CapCut deep dive: auto-captions, effects, transitions
- Filming with phones: lighting, audio, stability
- Edit your first short-form video

**Weekly Project:** "60-Second Banger"
- One polished short-form video ready for publishing
- Must include: Hook (first 3 sec), value/entertainment, CTA
- AI-generated captions required

**Tools Mastered:**
- CapCut (primary editor)
- Phone camera techniques

**Key Concepts:**
- Pattern interrupts every 5-8 seconds
- The micro three-act structure (Hook → Tension → Payoff)
- Sound/music as discovery mechanism

---

### WEEK 5: "Sound That Resonates"
**Theme:** Audio, Music, and Voice

**Learning Objectives:**
- Find and use trending sounds legally
- Generate AI music for content
- Create voiceovers with AI tools
- Edit audio for different platforms

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: How trending sounds boost discovery
- Finding trending audio:
  - TikTok: "Add Music" → trending tab
  - Instagram: Arrow icon next to audio
  - Tokchart.com for fastest-rising sounds
- AI music generation with Lami AI/Singify

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- Voiceover creation with ElevenLabs
- When to use AI voice vs. your voice
- Podcast/audio content basics

**Weekly Project:** "Audio-First Content"
Choose one:
- A TikTok using a trending sound creatively
- A 3-minute mini podcast with AI-generated intro/outro
- A video with AI voiceover

**Tools Introduced:**
- Lami AI (music, free)
- Singify (music, free)
- ElevenLabs (voice, free tier)
- Musicfy AI (voice covers, free)

---

### WEEK 6: "The Algorithm Whisperer"
**Theme:** How Platforms Actually Work

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand algorithm mechanics for each platform
- Identify what triggers shares, saves, comments
- Apply the STEPPS virality framework
- Optimize content for discovery

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "The Algorithm Doesn't Hate You" — It's just math
- TikTok's 4-stage distribution cascade (200 → 10K → 100K → viral)
- Signal hierarchy: Completion rate > Rewatches > Shares > Comments > Likes
- The STEPPS framework for virality

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- Platform-specific optimization:
  - TikTok: Say keywords aloud (audio indexing)
  - Instagram: Saves are gold, no TikTok watermarks
  - YouTube: Watch time is king
- Hashtag strategies that work (3-5, not 30)

**Weekly Project:** "Algorithm Audit + Optimized Content"
- Analyze 5 of your posts: What worked? Why?
- Create one piece optimized for algorithm using all principles learned
- Document your optimization choices

**Key Concepts:**
- STEPPS: Social Currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical Value, Stories
- High-arousal emotions spread; low-arousal dies
- Completion rate matters more than likes

### WEEK 7: "Your Creator Brand"
**Theme:** Standing Out in a Crowded Feed

**Learning Objectives:**
- Define personal creator brand and niche
- Create consistent visual identity with AI
- Develop recognizable voice and style
- Build cross-platform presence

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "Why Do You Follow Who You Follow?" — Brand analysis of favorite creators
- Finding your niche: Specific > General
- The 70/20/10 content rule:
  - 70% proven formats
  - 20% variations
  - 10% experimental

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- AI-assisted brand kit creation
- Profile optimization for each platform
- Content pillars: 3 topics you'll focus on

**Weekly Project:** "Complete Creator Brand Kit"
- Logo/profile image (AI-generated + polished)
- Color palette and visual style
- Bio optimized for each platform
- 3 content pillars defined
- First piece in your new brand style

**Tools Used:**
- Canva (brand kit)
- Bing Image Creator (logo concepts)
- ChatGPT (bio writing)

---

### WEEK 8: "Creating Together"
**Theme:** Collaboration and Community

**Learning Objectives:**
- Collaborate effectively with AI tools
- Give and receive creative feedback
- Build community through engagement
- Create collaborative content

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "Duet This" — The power of building on others' content
- Collaboration types: Duets, Stitches, Joint videos, Challenges
- Finding collaboration partners at your level
- How to pitch a collab

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- Peer feedback workshop: "I like... I wonder... I wish..."
- Community engagement strategies
- Responding to comments for algorithm boost

**Weekly Project:** "Collab Content"
- Create one piece with a classmate
- Can be: Duet, reaction, joint video, challenge participation
- Both creators must publish to their platforms

**Community Building Tactics:**
- Reply to EVERY comment in first hour
- Comment meaningfully on 20+ posts in your niche daily
- Create "save-worthy" content (tips, lists, guides)

---

### WEEK 9: "Data-Driven Creativity"
**Theme:** Analytics and Iteration

**Learning Objectives:**
- Read and interpret content analytics
- Use data to improve content decisions
- A/B test content variations
- Iterate on underperforming content

**Session 1 (90 min):**
- Hook: "Your Numbers Tell a Story" — Live analytics walkthrough
- Metrics that matter:
  - Watch completion rate (if low = hook problem)
  - Share rate (highest value engagement)
  - Save rate (long-term value indicator)
- Using AI to analyze performance patterns

**Session 2 (90 min):**
- A/B testing: Same content, different variables
- The iteration cycle: Create → Publish → Analyze → Improve
- Identifying what to double down on

**Weekly Project:** "Analytics Report + Iteration"
- Full analytics report on your published content
- AI-assisted analysis of patterns
- One underperforming piece, revised and republished
- Document what you changed and why

**Benchmarks:**
- Watch completion: >50% good, >70% great
- Engagement rate: 3-6% good, >6% great
- Share rate: >1% good, >3% great

---

## Ethics and Safety

### Core Principles

1. **Transparency** — Always disclose AI use when required
2. **Authenticity** — AI assists; YOU create
3. **Accuracy** — Verify all AI-generated information
4. **Privacy** — Never input personal information into AI tools
5. **Respect** — No misleading or harmful content

### Student Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use AI as creative assistant
- Fact-check AI outputs
- Credit AI tools when significant
- Choose tools with content filters

**DO NOT:**
- Input personal information
- Trust AI facts without verification
- Claim AI work as entirely yours
- Create misleading content

### Parental Consent

Required for:
- Platform account creation
- Content publishing
- AI tool usage
