# Sales Review Checklist -- Mandatory Pre-Advancement Verification

Every deal must pass this checklist before advancing stages.
Every quarter must pass this checklist before forecast submission.
No exceptions. No shortcuts.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS CHECKLIST

1. Complete the relevant section based on deal stage
2. Mark each item as PASS, FAIL, or N/A
3. Any FAIL in a critical item = deal cannot advance
4. All sections must be completed for quarterly reviews
5. Keep this checklist in the deal record (CRM or deal folder)

---

## SECTION 1: PRE-ENGAGEMENT CHECKLIST

Complete before first outreach or meeting.

### Account Research

- [ ] **ICP Fit Verified**: Company matches Ideal Customer Profile on firmographics (industry, size, revenue, geography)
- [ ] **Technographic Fit**: Current tech stack is compatible with our solution
- [ ] **Timing Signals Identified**: Trigger events documented (funding, leadership change, expansion, tech migration, competitor issues)
- [ ] **Account History Checked**: Prior engagement history reviewed in CRM (previous deals, churned, contacted before)
- [ ] **LinkedIn Research**: Key stakeholders identified with roles and tenure
- [ ] **Annual Report / Press Review**: Recent business priorities and challenges documented

### Outreach Preparation

- [ ] **Persona-Specific Messaging**: Outreach tailored to role and pain, not generic
- [ ] **Value Hypothesis Drafted**: Specific hypothesis about how we can help this account
- [ ] **Multi-Channel Plan**: Outreach planned across email, phone, LinkedIn, and referrals
- [ ] **Referral Path Explored**: Checked for mutual connections, customers in same industry, partner relationships
- [ ] **Competitive Landscape Noted**: Known competitors in the account identified

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] ICP fit is confirmed with data, not assumption
- [ ] Value hypothesis is specific to this account, not generic

## SECTION 2: DISCOVERY CHECKLIST

Complete after discovery calls before advancing to evaluation.

### SPIN Execution

- [ ] **Situation Questions Answered**: Current state fully documented (tools, process, team, budget, timeline)
- [ ] **Problem Questions Answered**: Prospect has stated specific problems in their own words
- [ ] **Implication Questions Explored**: Business impact of problems quantified (revenue loss, time waste, risk exposure)
- [ ] **Need-Payoff Articulated**: Prospect has described what solving the problem would mean for them
- [ ] **Direct Quotes Captured**: At least 3 prospect quotes documented verbatim

### Pain Quantification

- [ ] **Annual Cost of Problem**: Dollar amount documented
- [ ] **Source of Number**: Prospect-stated or co-calculated (not rep-estimated)
- [ ] **Cost of Inaction**: 12-month impact of doing nothing documented
- [ ] **Urgency Driver Identified**: Why this problem must be solved now (not later)
- [ ] **Budget Existence Confirmed**: Budget allocated, budget planned, or no budget (and path to create)

### MEDDPICC Foundation

- [ ] **Metrics**: Business outcomes the prospect wants to achieve, quantified
- [ ] **Economic Buyer**: Named and identified (even if not yet accessed)
- [ ] **Decision Criteria**: Initial understanding of how they will evaluate
- [ ] **Decision Process**: Initial understanding of approval steps
- [ ] **Champion Candidate**: At least one internal advocate identified

### Discovery Quality Gates

- [ ] At least 60% of talk time was the prospect speaking
- [ ] No product demo was given during discovery (discovery is about them, not you)
- [ ] Follow-up email sent within 24 hours summarizing what was learned
- [ ] Next meeting booked before ending the discovery call

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] Pain is quantified in dollars with a documented source
- [ ] At least one champion candidate identified
- [ ] Prospect has articulated the problem in their own words

## SECTION 3: EVALUATION / DEMO CHECKLIST

Complete before advancing to proposal.

### Demo Preparation

- [ ] **Demo Customized**: Demo script tailored to prospect's specific pain points and use cases
- [ ] **Relevant Personas Included**: Right stakeholders invited to the demo
- [ ] **Success Criteria Pre-Aligned**: What the prospect needs to see to move forward, agreed in advance
- [ ] **Competitive Traps Set**: Demo highlights capabilities competitors cannot match
- [ ] **Technical Environment Ready**: Demo environment configured, data populated, no risk of failure

### Technical Validation

- [ ] **Technical Requirements Documented**: Integration needs, security requirements, compliance needs
- [ ] **SE Engaged**: Solutions Engineer briefed and aligned on deal strategy
- [ ] **POC Criteria Defined**: If POC required, success criteria are specific and measurable
- [ ] **POC Time-Boxed**: POC has a defined end date (max 2 weeks recommended)
- [ ] **Technical Win Confirmed**: Prospect's technical team has validated the solution works

### Stakeholder Mapping

- [ ] **Org Chart Started**: Key players identified with roles
- [ ] **Champion Confirmed**: Champion has taken action on your behalf (not just "likes you")
- [ ] **Economic Buyer Identified**: Named individual with budget authority
- [ ] **EB Introduction Planned**: Champion has agreed to introduce you to EB (or explain why not)
- [ ] **Blockers Identified**: Anyone who could derail the deal is identified with mitigation plan
- [ ] **Multi-Threading Started**: At least 2 independent contacts engaged

### Competitive Assessment

- [ ] **Competitors Named**: All competitors in the evaluation identified
- [ ] **Battle Card Reviewed**: Competitive positioning prepared
- [ ] **Trap Questions Delivered**: Champion coached on questions to ask competitors
- [ ] **Differentiation Articulated**: Clear "why us" that is not just features

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] Champion has taken a concrete action on your behalf
- [ ] EB is identified and introduction is planned
- [ ] Technical validation is complete or in progress with defined criteria
- [ ] Deal is multi-threaded (2+ independent contacts)

## SECTION 4: PROPOSAL CHECKLIST

Complete before sending any proposal or commercial terms.

### Pre-Proposal Verification

- [ ] **Mutual Action Plan Agreed**: Customer and rep have agreed on steps, dates, and owners to close
- [ ] **Decision Criteria Confirmed**: Written list of what matters, ranked by priority
- [ ] **Decision Process Mapped**: Every approval step documented with named approvers and timeline
- [ ] **Paper Process Understood**: Legal, procurement, security review requirements known
- [ ] **Budget Confirmed**: Amount, source, fiscal year timing verified
- [ ] **EB Engaged**: Economic Buyer has been in at least one conversation
- [ ] **Competitive Position Clear**: You know where you stand vs. competitors

### Proposal Content

- [ ] **Pricing Aligned to Value**: Price justified by quantified business impact, not cost-plus
- [ ] **ROI Model Included**: Clear return on investment calculation using prospect's numbers
- [ ] **Implementation Plan Included**: Phased rollout with milestones and responsibilities
- [ ] **Success Criteria Defined**: Measurable outcomes that define a successful implementation
- [ ] **Terms Appropriate**: Payment terms, contract length, SLA match deal requirements
- [ ] **No Surprises**: Every element of the proposal has been discussed before sending

### Internal Approvals

- [ ] **Pricing Approved**: Discount level approved by appropriate authority
- [ ] **Legal Review**: Non-standard terms reviewed by legal
- [ ] **Deal Desk Review**: For deals >$100K, deal desk has approved structure
- [ ] **Executive Sponsor Briefed**: For strategic deals, exec sponsor is aware and engaged

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] Mutual action plan exists and is agreed by both parties
- [ ] EB has been directly engaged (not just "aware")
- [ ] No discount >20% without VP written approval
- [ ] Proposal contains no terms that haven't been discussed

## SECTION 5: NEGOTIATION CHECKLIST

Complete before finalizing terms.

### Negotiation Preparation

- [ ] **ZOPA Mapped**: Walk-away, target, and estimated buyer range documented
- [ ] **Concession Plan Prepared**: Ordered list of what you can give and what you will ask in return
- [ ] **Non-Negotiables Listed**: Terms that cannot change, pre-approved by leadership
- [ ] **Procurement Intel Gathered**: Buyer's negotiation style, typical demands, timeline pressures
- [ ] **Approval Chain Ready**: Internal approvals lined up for rapid response

### During Negotiation

- [ ] **Every Concession Traded**: No unilateral gives; every concession matched with a get
- [ ] **Concessions Decrease in Value**: Started with larger concessions, reduced over time
- [ ] **Price Not First Lever**: Payment terms, scope, timeline explored before discounting
- [ ] **Urgency Maintained**: Deadline preserved, not extended repeatedly
- [ ] **All Requests Documented**: Every ask from both sides written down

### Post-Negotiation

- [ ] **Final Terms Documented**: Complete summary of agreed terms
- [ ] **All Promises Verified**: Every verbal commitment reflected in contract
- [ ] **Legal Review Complete**: Contract reviewed for non-standard terms
- [ ] **Internal Approval Obtained**: Final terms approved by required authority level

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] ZOPA was documented before entering negotiation
- [ ] No discount exceeds authorized level without proper approval
- [ ] Every concession has a documented corresponding ask

---

## SECTION 6: CLOSE / HANDOFF CHECKLIST

Complete before recognizing the deal as closed-won.

### Contract Execution

- [ ] **Contract Signed**: Fully executed by authorized signatories on both sides
- [ ] **PO Received**: Purchase order or payment commitment received (if applicable)
- [ ] **Payment Terms Confirmed**: First payment date and method established
- [ ] **Start Date Agreed**: Implementation kickoff date confirmed

### Handoff Preparation

- [ ] **Handoff Document Complete**: All sections filled (see SalesScore.md, Section 8)
- [ ] **Success Criteria Documented**: Customer's definition of success in their own words
- [ ] **Key Contacts Transferred**: Implementation stakeholders on customer side identified
- [ ] **Promises Register Complete**: Every commitment from sales process documented and verified
- [ ] **Risk Register Complete**: Known risks from sales process with mitigation plans
- [ ] **Technical Requirements Transferred**: Integration specs, data migration needs, security requirements

### Handoff Meeting

- [ ] **Meeting Scheduled**: Sales, CS, and implementation team all attending
- [ ] **Customer Invited**: Customer stakeholders attending kickoff portion
- [ ] **30-Day Milestones Defined**: First month objectives agreed
- [ ] **Escalation Path Established**: Both internal and customer-side escalation contacts named
- [ ] **Communication Cadence Set**: Regular check-in schedule agreed

### CRM Hygiene

- [ ] **Deal Updated**: Stage, amount, close date, competitors all accurate
- [ ] **Notes Complete**: Full deal history documented in CRM
- [ ] **Win Reason Documented**: Why the customer chose us (in their words)
- [ ] **Revenue Recognized**: Deal properly recorded for quota and compensation

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] Handoff document is complete with all sections
- [ ] Handoff meeting is scheduled with all required parties
- [ ] All promises from sales process are documented and verified against contract

## SECTION 7: QUARTERLY REVIEW CHECKLIST

Complete before each quarterly business review.

### Pipeline Health

- [ ] **Pipeline Coverage**: 3x+ pipeline to quota ratio verified
- [ ] **Stage Distribution**: Healthy distribution across stages (not bottom-heavy)
- [ ] **Pipeline Age**: No deals older than 2x average sales cycle without explanation
- [ ] **Close Date Accuracy**: <15% of deals had close dates pushed in the quarter
- [ ] **Stale Deals Cleaned**: Deals with no activity >30 days reviewed and dispositioned

### Forecast Accuracy

- [ ] **Commit Accuracy**: Commit forecast vs. actual within +/- 10%
- [ ] **Best Case Accuracy**: Best case vs. actual within +/- 20%
- [ ] **Upside Tracking**: Upside deals that closed tracked for pattern analysis
- [ ] **Slip Analysis**: All deals that slipped from commit analyzed for root cause

### Win/Loss Analysis

- [ ] **Win Reviews Completed**: All closed-won deals >$25K reviewed
- [ ] **Loss Reviews Completed**: All closed-lost deals >$25K reviewed with post-mortem
- [ ] **Win Themes Identified**: Common factors in wins documented
- [ ] **Loss Themes Identified**: Common factors in losses documented with action plans
- [ ] **Competitive Win Rate**: Win rate by competitor tracked and trended

### Activity Metrics

- [ ] **Activity Volume**: Calls, meetings, proposals on track
- [ ] **Conversion Rates**: Stage-to-stage conversion rates tracked and trended
- [ ] **Cycle Time**: Average sales cycle length tracked by segment
- [ ] **ASP Trend**: Average selling price trend analyzed (growing/shrinking)

### Critical Items (Must Pass)

- [ ] All closed-lost deals >$25K have completed post-mortems
- [ ] Forecast accuracy is within acceptable variance
- [ ] Pipeline coverage is at 3x or above with explanation if below

---

## CHECKLIST ENFORCEMENT

- This checklist is mandatory, not advisory
- "I forgot" is not an acceptable reason for skipping items
- Critical items must ALL pass for stage advancement
- Incomplete checklists are flagged in weekly pipeline review
- Repeated checklist failures trigger performance discussion
