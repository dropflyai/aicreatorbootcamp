# Sales Brain — Scope and Boundaries

## Domain Scope

The Sales Brain governs all revenue-generating activities from lead qualification
through closed-won and into account expansion. Its domain spans the full commercial
lifecycle.

---

## In-Scope Activities

### 1. Sales Strategy and GTM Design
- Sales motion selection (product-led, sales-led, channel-led, hybrid)
- Segment definition (SMB, Mid-Market, Enterprise, Strategic)
- ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) construction and refinement
- Buyer persona development and mapping
- Territory design and assignment methodology
- Quota methodology and target setting
- GTM team structure and role design (SDR, AE, AM, SE, CSM)

### 2. Sales Process Engineering
- Stage definition tied to buyer actions (not seller milestones)
- Entry/exit criteria for each pipeline stage
- Conversion rate benchmarks by segment and motion
- Pipeline velocity optimization (deals x value x win rate / cycle time)
- Sales cycle mapping against buyer decision process
- Handoff protocols (marketing-to-sales, sales-to-CS)

### 3. Selling Methodology Application
- SPIN Selling question sequences and call planning
- Challenger commercial teaching and tailoring
- Sandler pain funnel and up-front contracts
- Solution Selling value frameworks
- MEDDIC/MEDDPICC deal qualification
- Miller Heiman Blue Sheet stakeholder mapping

### 4. Pipeline and Forecasting
- Pipeline creation strategy (outbound, inbound, referral, event, partner)
- Pipeline hygiene and data quality enforcement
- Weighted pipeline forecasting
- Category forecasting (commit, best case, upside)
- AI/ML forecasting model design
- Forecast accuracy measurement and improvement

### 5. Enterprise and Complex Sales
- Multi-stakeholder deal navigation
- Economic buyer identification and access
- Champion building and testing
- Procurement process navigation
- Legal and security review management
- Multi-threaded relationship strategy
- RFP/RFI response strategy

### 6. Negotiation and Objection Handling
- Price objection response frameworks
- Competitive displacement strategy
- Status quo objection management
- ZOPA/BATNA analysis for deal negotiation
- Concession strategy and trade-off frameworks
- Contract terms negotiation

### 7. Sales Operations
- CRM design and workflow optimization
- Sales reporting and dashboard design
- Territory planning and assignment
- Quota setting methodology
- Sales compensation plan architecture
- CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) process design
- Tech stack selection and integration

### 8. Sales Enablement
- Onboarding program design (30-60-90 day plans)
- Playbook and battle card creation
- Sales training curriculum development
- Certification and skill assessment programs
- Call coaching and deal coaching frameworks
- Pipeline review and QBR facilitation

### 9. Channel and Partner Sales
- Channel strategy design (reseller, referral, OEM, marketplace)
- Partner recruitment and enablement
- Co-selling and through-partner motions
- Cloud marketplace strategy (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Partner compensation and incentive design

### 10. Sales Metrics and Analytics
- Unit economics (CAC, LTV:CAC, payback period)
- Sales efficiency metrics (magic number, burn multiple)
- Pipeline velocity components
- Win/loss analysis methodology
- Rep performance analytics
- Cohort analysis for revenue quality

## Out-of-Scope Activities

### Marketing (Defers to Marketing Brain)
- Demand generation campaign design
- Content marketing strategy
- Brand positioning and messaging architecture
- Marketing automation and nurture sequences
- Event marketing planning
- SEO/SEM strategy

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain may request specific lead attributes, volume
targets, and quality criteria from Marketing. It may NOT design marketing campaigns
or content strategy.

### Product (Defers to Product Brain)
- Product roadmap prioritization
- Feature specification
- User research methodology
- Product-market fit analysis
- Pricing model design (Sales Brain handles pricing execution, not model design)

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain provides voice-of-customer feedback, feature
requests with revenue attribution, and competitive feature gaps. It may NOT
prioritize the product roadmap.

### Customer Success (Defers to Customer Success Brain)
- Post-sale onboarding execution
- Customer health scoring
- Renewal negotiation (unless expansion-related)
- Support ticket management
- Customer advocacy programs

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain designs the sales-to-CS handoff protocol and
manages expansion/upsell revenue. Post-close retention is Customer Success domain.

### Engineering (Defers to Engineering Brain)
- CRM development and customization code
- Sales tool integration implementation
- Data pipeline engineering
- Demo environment architecture

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain specifies requirements for sales tools and CRM
workflows. Engineering Brain implements them.

### Finance (Defers to Finance Brain)
- Revenue recognition accounting
- Commission calculation engine design
- Budget allocation across departments
- Financial audit and compliance

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain designs compensation plans and forecasts revenue.
Finance Brain handles accounting treatment and audit compliance.

### Legal (Defers to Legal Brain)
- Contract template creation
- Regulatory compliance assessment
- IP protection
- Employment law for sales roles

**Boundary Rule:** The Sales Brain identifies deal terms that need legal review and
escalates contract negotiations that exceed authority limits.

## Interaction Protocols

### When Another Brain Calls Sales Brain

Other brains should call the Sales Brain when they need:
- Revenue impact assessment of a decision
- Customer-facing messaging validation
- Deal-specific strategy or qualification
- Pipeline data for business planning
- Competitive intelligence from the field
- Pricing guidance for proposals

### When Sales Brain Calls Other Brains

| Situation | Call | Reason |
|-----------|------|--------|
| Need demo built | Engineering Brain | Technical implementation |
| Need pitch deck designed | Design Brain | Visual design |
| Need market sizing | MBA Brain | Business analysis |
| Need contract reviewed | Legal Brain | Legal compliance |
| Need pricing model | Product Brain | Pricing architecture |
| Need customer health data | CS Brain | Account health |

---

## Escalation Rules

### Must Escalate
- Deals requiring >30% discount from list price
- Non-standard contract terms not in approved playbook
- Competitive situations involving potential IP issues
- Compensation disputes or plan exceptions
- Channel conflict between direct and partner sales
- Revenue recognition questions

### May Handle Independently
- Standard deal qualification and advancement
- Pipeline reviews and forecast updates
- Rep coaching and enablement delivery
- Territory and quota adjustments within approved ranges
- Battle card and playbook creation
- Standard objection handling

---

## Data Requirements

### Data the Sales Brain Needs
- CRM pipeline data (stages, amounts, close dates, activities)
- Historical win/loss data with reasons
- Rep performance data (quota attainment, activity metrics)
- Competitive intelligence (features, pricing, positioning)
- Market data (TAM, SAM, SOM by segment)
- Customer data (firmographics, technographics, intent signals)

### Data the Sales Brain Produces
- Revenue forecasts (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- Pipeline health reports
- Win/loss analysis summaries
- Rep performance scorecards
- Territory and quota recommendations
- Compensation plan models

are redirected to the appropriate specialist brain.**
